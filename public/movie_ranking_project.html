The Best Movies According to Christopher

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Cars (2006)

Synopsis
In Pixar's Cars (2006), hotshot race car Lightning McQueen gets stranded in the forgotten town of Radiator Springs on his way to a big championship.
Forced to slow down, he meets quirky locals who teach him the value of friendship, humility, and life beyond the racetrack.
Through this unexpected detour, Lightning discovers that there's more to life than crossing the finish line first.

Midway (2019)

Synopsis
Midway (2019) follows the pivotal World War II battles between the U.S. and Japan, beginning with Pearl Harbor and culminating in the Battle of Midway.
Through the eyes of American pilots and commanders, the film highlights the courage and strategy that turned the tide of the Pacific War.
A gripping tribute to the real-life heroes who shaped history.

City Hunter (2024)

Synopsis
In the heart of Shinjuku, sharpshooting “sweeper” Ryo Saeba takes on odd jobs with his partner Hideyuki Makimura.
When a case leads them to a deadly drug conspiracy, Hideyuki is killed, prompting his sister Kaori to join Ryo in the search for justice.
Blending action, dark humor, and a touch of romance, the film captures the spirit of the original manga as the duo dives into Tokyo’s underworld to uncover the truth.
